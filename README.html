Overview:

After watching the superbowl this year, I had a few takeaways. First, as a Patriots fan, this is going to be a sad offseason. Second, the commercials featured a lot of AI content. These initial impressions of the superbowl commercials ignited a deeper curiosity, which is the motivation for my project. This project analyzes recent technology-related news articles to understand how often tech related news has the words “AI”.

Using data collected from the source NewsAPI, this project uses a small data pipeline that retrieves article data, cleans, and visualizes it for the audience. The contents produced contain AI usage in recent publications online.

Problem Framing: This project focuses on three main areas: How frequently is technology news published over the past few weeks? How often does the words artificial intelligence appear in technology news headlines Which news sources produce the most

Data Preparation:

Collected from NewsAPI, which returns news article information in a raw JSON. Only the fields needed for the analysis were kept. Each article was reduced to its source, title, publication time, and author. I wanted to keep the dataset relevant to my project prompt. Next, the publication time was cleaned by converting it from a text string into a data and time format. By doing this, I could group articles by day or month and compare how often technology news was published over time. Finally, article tiles were examined for the “AI”.
